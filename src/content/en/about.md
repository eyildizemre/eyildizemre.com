As I said above, there's more to me than CS. There's a lot I want to share that isn't software. That's why I designed WORKS as a superset — a place to show everything I make. Click a project there and you'll be taken to wherever it belongs: if it's software, to the CODE page; if it's a poem or collection, to the "şiirler" page; if it's a story, to "öykü"...
